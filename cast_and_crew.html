Cast & Crew

Eddard “Ned” Stark
PLAYED BY SEAN BEAN
Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. The childhood friend of Robert Baratheon, he was a key player in Robert's rebelliFon, helping him win the throne from the Targaryens. He is married to Catelyn Tully.
Robert Baratheon
PLAYED BY MARK ADDY
King of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros and longtime friend of Ned Stark. Robert was engaged to Ned's sister, Lyanna, whose abduction by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen incited Robert's uprising against the throne. When Lyanna died during the war, Robert married Cersei Lannister.
Tyrion Lannister
PLAYED BY PETER DINKLAGE
Called "The Imp," because of his small stature, he is the youngest son of Tywin Lannister, the richest lord in the Seven Kingdoms and younger brother to Queen Cersei and Ser Jaime Lannister. What he lacks in height, he makes up in wit.
Cersei Lannister
PLAYED BY LENA HEADEY
Queen of the Seven Kingdoms and wife of King Robert. Fiercely protective of her three children, she also has an unusually tight connection to her twin brother, Jaime..
Catelyn Stark
PLAYED BY MICHELLE FAIRLEY
Wife of Ned Stark, born Catelyn of House Tully, the ruling house of the Riverlands. Originally engaged to Ned’s older brother Brandon, she married Ned when he was killed.
Jaime Lannister
PLAYED BY NIKOLAJ COSTER-WALDAU
The Queen's twin brother. It was he who murdered the previous King (Aerys), despite have sworn to protect him. This deed earned him his nickname: “Kingslayer.”
Daenerys Targaryen
PLAYED BY EMILIA CLARKE
Princess of House Targaryen, living in exile in Essos with her brother, Viserys.
Viserys Targaryen
PLAYED BY HARRY LLOYD
Prince of House Targaryen, whose father, the "Mad" King Aerys was usurped by King Robert. Viserys lives in self-exile with his sister Daenerys and is obsessed with regaining his family's throne.
Jon Snow
PLAYED BY KIT HARINGTON
Ned's bastard son. He joins the Night’s Watch, following in the footsteps of his Uncle Benjen. His direwolf is named Ghost.
Robb Stark
PLAYED BY RICHARD MADDEN
The oldest of Ned and Cat's five children. His direwolf is named Grey Wind.
Sansa Stark
PLAYED BY SOPHIE TURNER
Ned and Cat's oldest daughter. Demure and ladylike, her dream is to marry Prince Joffrey. Her direwolf is named Lady.
Arya Stark
PLAYED BY MAISIE WILLIAMS
The younger of Ned and Cat's two daughters. A tomboy, she would rather fence than dance. Her direwolf is named Nymeria, after a warrior queen.
Bran Stark
PLAYED BY ISAAC HEMPSTEAD-WRIGHT
Ned and Cat's younger son, who has an inquisitive nature and a penchant for climbing Winterfell's walls. His direwolf is named Summer.
Rickon Stark
PLAYED BY ART PARKINSON
Rickon is the youngest of the Stark children. His direwolf is named Shaggydog.
Joffrey Baratheon
PLAYED BY JACK GLEESON
The oldest of King Robert and Queen Cersei's children.
Jorah Mormont
PLAYED BY IAIN GLEN
A knight now in exile in Essos who advises Viserys and Daenerys Targaryen as they make a life among the Dothraki.
Theon Greyjoy
PLAYED BY ALFIE ALLEN
Ned's ward, heir to House Greyjoy. He was brought to Winterfell after his father, Balon Greyjoy, tried to rise against King Robert. Although raised among the Stark children, he is keenly aware he is not one of them.
Petyr Baelish (“Littlefinger”)
PLAYED BY AIDAN GILLEN
Master of Coin and member of the King’s Small Council, he grew up with Catelyn Stark for whom he still harbors feelings. Beyond his official duties, he is the eyes and ears of King's Landing along with Varys.
Sandor Clegane (“The Hound”)
PLAYED BY RORY MCCANN
Personal bodyguard to Prince Joffrey, half his face is covered in burn scars as a result of his brother thrusting him into a fire.
Samwell Tarly
PLAYED BY JOHN BRADLEY
An overweight and timid, Night’s Watch recruit who is forced to join after being disinherited by his family. He falls under Jon Snow's protection and becomes his best friend.
Renly Baratheon
PLAYED BY GETHIN ANTHONY
King Robert’s youngest brother, Master of Laws on the King’s Small Council and Lord of Storm’s End. He is a close friend of Loras Tyrell, the Knight of Flowers.
Ros
PLAYED BY ESME BIANCO
A prostitute from the North who journeyed to King's Landing.
Jeor Mormont
PLAYED BY JAMES COSMO
Father of the exiled Jorah Mormont and former lord of Bear Island. He is now the lord commander of the Night’s Watch and a friend to Jon Snow.
Gendry
PLAYED BY JOE DEMPSIE
An apprentice smith in King’s Landing who is of interest to Jon Arryn and Ned Stark.
Lysa Arryn
PLAYED BY KATE DICKIE
Catelyn Stark's younger sister and the wife of Jon Arryn, the Hand of the King. Both she and Catelyn were raised with Petyr Baelish.
Robin Arryn
PLAYED BY LINO FACIOLI
Lysa and Jon Arryn's son. He has not yet been weaned from his mother.
Bronn
PLAYED BY JEROME FLYNN
A mercenary who joins Tyrion Lannister and becomes his right-hand man.
Grand Maester Pycelle
PLAYED BY JULIAN GLOVER
The aged maester of King’s Landing and a member of the Small Council.
Varys
PLAYED BY CONLETH HILL
A eunuch and a member of the Small Council. A master of disguise, he along with Littlefinger are always aware of what goes on in Court.
Loras Tyrell
PLAYED BY FINN JONES
A handsome and popular young knight, referred to as the Knight of Flowers, Loras is a close friend of Renly Barthenon.
Shae
PLAYED BY SIBEL KEKILLI
A prostitute-turned-mistress of Tyrion Lannister.
Benjen Stark
PLAYED BY JOSEPH MAWLE
Ned's younger brother and First Ranger of the Night Watch, he is a hero of Jon Snow's.
Barristan Selmy
PLAYED BY IAN MCELHINNEY
As a member of the Kingsguard under the former king, Aerys, he never wavered in his oath to protect. For his commitment, Robert pardoned him and promoted him to Lord Commander.
Khal Drogo
PLAYED BY JASON MOMOA
The warrior leader of a vast tribe of horseman in Essos.
Hodor
PLAYED BY KRISTIAN NAIRN
One of Winterfell many loyal servants. Simple-minded and oversized, Hodor will serve as Bran's primary method of transport.
Lancel Lannister
PLAYED BY EUGENE SIMON
Robert's squire. A first cousin of Queen Cersei, he bears some resemblance to her brother Jaime.
Maester Luwin
PLAYED BY DONALD SUMPTER
Maester of Winterfell, he provides wise counsel to Ned and serves as tutor to the children.
Alliser Thorne
PLAYED BY OWEN TEALE
Master-at-arms of Castle Black, it is his job to turn the undisciplined and obstinate recruits into rangers of the Night Watch. He resents Jon Snow.
Osha
PLAYED BY NATALIA TENA
A wildling woman who helps raise Bran.
Maester Aemon
PLAYED BY PETER VAUGHAN
The elderly, blind maester of the Night's Watch who is sympathetic to Jon Snow's plight.

Season 2 characters

Talisa Stark
PLAYED BY OONA CHAPLIN
A native of Volantis, Talisa is a healer.
Brienne of Tarth
PLAYED BY GWENDOLINE CHRISTIE
Brienne is a highborn lady who would rather be a knight. As Catelyn Stark's envoy, she is tasked with escorting Jaime Lannister back to King's Landing.
Davos Seaworth
PLAYED BY LIAM CUNNINGHAM
A reformed smuggler, it was Davos who provided Stannis' forces much-needed provisions during the long siege of Storm's End.
Tywin Lannister
PLAYED BY CHARLES DANCE
Father of Cersei, Jaime and Tyrion, he is the most powerful lord in the Seven Kingdoms, because of his enormous wealth. His wife died giving birth to Tyrion, hence his hatred for him.
Stannis Baratheon
PLAYED BY STEPHEN DILLANE
A serious man who preferred the solitude of the family seat in Dragonstone to King's Landing, Stannis is still challenging his nephew Joffrey for the Iron Throne.
Margaery Tyrell
PLAYED BY NATALIE DORMER
Mace Tyrell's only daughter, Margaery is as clever as she is beautiful.
Ygritte
PLAYED BY ROSE LESLIE
A wildling woman that Jon Snow encountered traveling beyond the Wall, Ygritte is fiercely proud of her free status. Her opinions have clouded Jon’s stance about the Night’s Watch.
Balon Greyjoy
PLAYED BY PATRICK MALAHIDE
Head of the ruling house of the Iron Islands, Balon Greyjoy lost two of his sons in a failed revolt against Robert Baratheon. His only surviving son, Theon, was raised as a ward by the Starks.
Roose Bolton
PLAYED BY MICHAEL MCELHATTON
Lord of the Dreadfort and head of House Bolton, Roose takes pride in his family's sigil of a flayed man.
Gilly
PLAYED BY HANNAH MURRAY
A wildling from Craster’s Keep, Gilly and her son Sam journeyed south of the Wall under the protection of Sam Tarly.
Podrick Payne
PLAYED BY DANIEL PORTMAN
Quiet and unassuming, Podrick is Tyrion’s squire.
Melisandre
PLAYED BY CARICE VAN HOUTEN
A Red Priestess from Asshai, Melisandre worships the Lord of Light. She believes her visions will lead her to the true king of Westeros.
Yara Greyjoy
PLAYED BY GEMMA WHELAN
Theon's older sister, she is the only child of Balon Greyjoy remaining on the Iron Islands.
Jaqen H’ghar
PLAYED BY TOM WLASCHIHA
Jaqen helped Arya escape from Harrenhal and provided her with an iron coin to ensure her safe passage to Braavos

Season 3 characters

Grey Worm
PLAYED BY JACOB ANDERSON
One of the highly-trained Unsullied fighters from Astapor, he was named by his fellow soldiers as their leader when they were set free by Daenerys Targaryen.
Beric Dondarrion
PLAYED BY RICHARD DORMER
Lord Beric of Blackhaven, he was tasked by Ned Stark, then Hand of the King, to bring Gregor Clegane to justice. A follower of the Red faith, Beric has been brought back to life several times by Thoros of Myr.
Missandei
PLAYED BY NATHALIE EMMANUEL
Formerly a slave in Astapor, Missandei is fluent in the Common Tongue and High Valyrian.
Mance Rayder
PLAYED BY CIARAN HINDS
Once a brother of the Night’s Watch, Mance abandoned his vows to join the wildlings. He serves as their King Beyond the Wall.
Tormund
PLAYED BY KRISTOFER HIVJU
A legendary wildling leader who supports Mance Rayder, Tormund is a fair and formidable warrior.
Ramsay Snow
PLAYED BY IWAN RHEON
A bastard son of Roose Bolton, Ramsay's bloodlust is even stronger than his father's.
Olenna Tyrell
PLAYED BY DIANA RIGG
Matriarch of House Tyrell, Lady Olenna is not shy about sharing her opinions. Although fond of her granddaughter Margaery, she feels less generous toward the men in her family.
Thoros of Myr
PLAYED BY PAUL KAYE
A native of the Free City of Myr and a famous soldier, he has since joined the outlaw group the Brotherhood Without Banners. Although Thoros serves as a priest for the Lord of Light, he is fond of drink.
Orell
PLAYED BY MACKENZIE CROOK
A wildling scout, he travels with an eagle.
Qyburn
PLAYED BY ANTON LESSER
Although trained in the medical arts, Qyburn was censured by the Citadel for his unauthorized experiments and does not wear the chains of a maester.
Brynden Tully (“The Blackfish”)
PLAYED BY CLIVE RUSSELL
Brynden is known as “the Blackfish,” due to the strained relationship between him and his older brother Hoster. Alongside Barristan Selmy, Brynden showed great heroism during the War of the Ninepenny Kings, a rebellion in the East that threatened the Seven Kingdoms.

Season 4 characters

Tommen Baratheon
PLAYED BY DEAN-CHARLES CHAPMAN
The youngest of Cersei’s children, Tommen inherited the throne after the death of his brother Joffrey.
Daario Naharis
PLAYED BY MICHIEL HUISMAN
Daenerys' advisor and lover.
Oberyn Martell
PLAYED BY PEDRO PASCAL
A prince of Dorne, Oberyn Martell is also a fierce fighter who earned the nickname, the "Red Viper."
Ellaria Sand
PLAYED BY INDIRA VARMA
Prince Oberyn Martell’s paramour.

Season 5 characters

Myrcella Baratheon
PLAYED BY NELL TIGER FREE
Fair-haired and demure, Myrcella now lives in Dorne as part of a deal her uncle Tyrion made with House Martell to align their houses and keep the princess safe.
Obara Sand
PLAYED BY KEISHA CASTLE-HUGHES
The eldest of Oberyn Martell's bastard daughters, Obara is as dangerous with a spear as he was.
Nym Sand
PLAYED BY JESSICA HENWICK
Half-Dornish and elegant, she uses the whip as her weapon of choice.
Tyene Sand
PLAYED BY ROSABELL LAURENTI SELLERS
The youngest of the Sand Snakes, Tyene is proficient with daggers.
High Sparrow
PLAYED BY JONATHAN PRYCE
The High Sparrow leads a group of religious zealots determined to combat vice in King's Landing.
Trystane Martell
PLAYED BY TOBY SEBASTIAN
A prince of Dorne and heir to Doran Martell, Trystane is engaged to Myrcella Baratheon.
Doran Martell
PLAYED BY ALEXANDER SIDDIG
A firm and diplomatic leader, Doran stayed largely neutral in the War of Five Kings.

Season 6 characters

Euron Greyjoy
PLAYED BY PILOU ASBÆK
The younger brother of Balon Greyjoy, he has recently returned to Pyke to take the Salt Throne.
Lady Crane
PLAYED BY ESSIE DAVIS
An actor with a Braavosi traveling company, she plays Cersei Lannister in their production of “The Bloody Hand.”
High Priestess
PLAYED BY SOUAD FARESS
The leader of the Dosh Khaleen, and herself the widow of a great khal, she was among those present when Daenerys ate a stallion’s heart.
Randyll Tarly
PLAYED BY JAMES FAULKNER
A legendary soldier, Randyll Tarly has no patience for his son Samwell’s studious nature. He forced Sam to join the Night’s Watch, making his second son Dickon heir to Horn Hill.
Izembaro
PLAYED BY RICHARD E. GRANT
The playwright of a traveling company who wrote and directs “The Bloody Hand,” a theatrical production of The War of Five Kings currently running in Braavos. Izembaro also stars as Robert Baratheon and Tywin Lannister.
Brother Ray
PLAYED BY IAN MCSHANE
A former soldier haunted by the atrocities he committed on the battlefield, Brother Ray is a septon of boundless compassion and kindness.

Season 7 characters

Archmaester Ebrose
PLAYED BY JIM BROADBENT
A Citadel archmaester, Ebrose is highly knowledgeable in medicine and the human body. He oversees several of Samwell Tarly’s duties, and is stern but affectionate to the new trainee.
